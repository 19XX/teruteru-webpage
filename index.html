Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the Settings tab and tap on "Restore Purchases". If you're still having trouble, please contact us.
How can I delete my account?
You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app. Go to Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent.
Why did my image generation fail?
Generation may fail due to content safety filters or temporary server load. Credits are usually not deducted for failed attempts. If you lost credits, please email us with your user ID.
