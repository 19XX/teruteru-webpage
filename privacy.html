Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Teruteru ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together follows:

  • Identity Data: User ID, Email address (via Apple/Google Sign-In).
  • Content Data:
    • Reference Images: Photos you upload are temporarily stored to process your request via Google Gemini AI. They are automatically deleted from our storage immediately after generation.
    • Generated Images: Images created by the AI are stored in your history.
  • Device Data: Device model, operating system version, and a unique device fingerprint. This is strictly used to prevent abuse of our free credit system and is not used for tracking or advertising.

3. Face Data Policy

We take the privacy of your face data very seriously. In compliance with Apple's guidelines, we provide the following specific information regarding our use of face data:

  • What face data do we collect?
    We collect user-uploaded reference images that may contain faces. These images are uploaded by you specifically for the purpose of using our "Image Generation Presets" (e.g., ID Photo, Wedding, Travel).
  • How do we use this face data?
    The face data is used exclusively to generate new, stylized images based on the selected preset. The AI analyzes the facial features in your reference images to create a new image that maintains your identity within the chosen theme. We do not use this data for any other purpose, such as marketing, advertising, or training general AI models.
  • Will the data be shared with any third parties?
    Your reference images are securely transmitted to Google Gemini AI solely for the purpose of image generation. We do not share, sell, or disclose your face data to any other third parties.
  • How long is face data retained?
    Reference Images: Images you upload are processed temporarily and are deleted immediately from the AI processing server (Google Files API) after the generation is complete. They are also removed from our temporary storage once the process finishes.
    Generated Images: The final images created by the AI are stored in your private history for 3 months, after which they are automatically deleted.

4. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To provide the AI image generation service you requested.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To improve our app and customer experience.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for.

  • Generated Images: Automatically deleted from our servers after 3 months.
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our app:

  • Supabase: For authentication and database hosting.
  • Google Gemini AI: For processing image generation requests.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data. You can delete your account directly within the app (Settings > Delete Account).
